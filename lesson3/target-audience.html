Target Audience:

My target audience really is just anybody needing to know the weather in a certain city, or who would like to purchase a weather related article of clothing and/or object.


Two Personas:

  • 20 year old, single woman, making minimum wage.
  • 30 year old, married woman, stay at home mother.

5 Scenarios:

Scenario #1:

Family going on a vacation. Mom wants to be prepared so she checks the weather and possibly even purchases necessary weather related objects for the vacation.


Scenario #2:

Young woman has a party that weekend, and wants to make sure the weather will be okay.


Scenario #3:

Man just moved to Seattle, and realizes he needs to purchase an umbrella.


Scenario #4:

Young man being forced to go to their extended family picnic, wanting to find any excuse not to go, so he checks the weather.


Scenario #1:

60 year old man, who needs to know the weather everyday so they can talk about it, but the newspaper didn't come that day.
