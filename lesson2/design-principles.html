Visual Hierarchy Cinemark cinemark.com They want you to see their promotions very first, which is why it is much bigger than anything else. The next biggest and most distinguishable things are the movies! This places clear emphasis on their promotions and their movies.
Hick's Law Nike nike.com As you can see, they don't have too many options. And they have split options into categories. This way the user doesn't get overwhelmed when making a selection.
Fitt's Law Target target.com In the categories section, they made sure to have each category/button huge and even has a picture with it. This makes these buttons easy and natural to click.
